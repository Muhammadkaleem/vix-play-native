# Vision & Scope

## Vision
A media player that plays *everything*, feels modern, and gets out of the way. Where MX Player has become cluttered with content portals and aggressive ads, this app stays player-first, respectful, and deeply customizable.

## Target users
- **Power users / tinkerers** — want remappable gestures, codec control, subtitle sync, network streaming.
- **Media hoarders** — large local libraries, MKV/AVI files, external subtitles, SMB/NAS shares.
- **Everyday viewers** — downloaded shows/movies, want it to "just play" with a clean UI.

## Differentiation vs MX Player
1. **Player-first home** — no OTT/games portal, no content feed.
2. **Fully remappable gestures** — any action to any zone, precision seek, variable-speed hold.
3. **Modern theming** — Material You dynamic color, true AMOLED, ambient backglow, player skins.
4. **AI subtitles** — on-device auto-generated subtitles (Whisper), auto intro/silence skip.
5. **Cross-device resume** — remembers position across devices (optional account).
6. **Respectful monetization** — calm free tier, one-time Pro unlock.

## In scope (v1)
Local video + audio playback, folder & library browsing, external + online subtitles, full gesture system, theming system, network streaming (HLS/DASH/SMB), Chromecast + DLNA, PiP + background audio, equalizer, private folder, Android TV.

## Out of scope (v1)
Video editing beyond trim, social features, built-in content catalog, live TV/IPTV management (P2 later), desktop/iOS ports.

## Success metrics
- Time-to-first-frame < 500ms for local files
- Crash-free sessions > 99.5%
- D30 retention competitive with category leaders
- Pro conversion > 2% of active users
